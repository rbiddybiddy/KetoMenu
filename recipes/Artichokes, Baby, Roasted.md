## Artichokes, Baby, Roasted

- 2 baby artichokes
- ½ lemon
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- ½ tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp black pepper
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp grated Parmesan, Comté or similar
- optional: ½ tbsp chopped fresh parsley

Remove tough outer leaves from artichokes.

Cut off & trim stems, and use a peeler to remove the tough outer layer.

Rub all cut surfaces with lemon as you work so they don’t turn brown when cooked.

Cut off the top ~1/3 of each artichoke to remove tough leaf-ends, then split down the middle.

Mix oil, vinegar, salt, pepper and garlic.

Place artichoke halves cut-side-up in a baking dish and drizzle with the oil mixture. Roast at 400º until tender, about 20 min.

Serve with cheese & parsley sprinkled on top.
