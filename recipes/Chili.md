## Chili

- 3 tbsp oil
- 1 lb meat chunks
- 1 lg onion, chopped
- 1 tbsp minced garlic
- 2 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp oregano
- 1 tsp salt
- 15 oz can diced tomatoes
- 1 lb dry beans, optionally pre-soaked
- 1 cup water (may need more – adjust)

Brown meat in oil in large pot over med-high heat, 5-10 min.

Add onion and cook 3-5 min until soft & golden.

Add garlic & spices and cook 1 min.

Add tomatoes, beans & water. Cover & simmer until beans are done.
