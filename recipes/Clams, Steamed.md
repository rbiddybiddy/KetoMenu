## Clams, Steamed

- 2 dozen clams
- some olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, pressed
- half a small onion, chopped
- 1/4 cup white wine
- 1 cup water
- parsley, salt, pepper
- 2 tbsp melted butter

Heat oil in large saucepan that has a cover.

Wash clams thoroughly, removing any blemishes. Discard any that have an odor.

Cook the garlic & onion until mushy. Add the wine and let cook for a couple of minutes to combine flavors.

Add the clams and water, cover and cook for about 10 minutes until clams open.

Transfer clams to serving dishes, discarding any that didn't open.

Add remaining ingredients to the pan to make a sauce, and pour over the clams.
