## Jambalaya
6 servings

- 1 med onion, chopped
- 1 green pepper, chopped
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 lb fully cooked sausage in ¼-inch slices
- 1 28-oz can diced tomatoes
- 3 cups water
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp paprika
- ½ tsp thyme
- ½ tsp oregano
- ¼ tsp garlic powder
- 1½ tsp salt
- 3 drops hot sauce
- 1½ cups uncooked rice

In soup pot, sauté onion and green pepper in oil until tender.

Stir in sausage, tomatoes, water, sugar and seasonings.

Bring to a boil and add rice.

Cover and cook until rice is tender, about 30 min.
