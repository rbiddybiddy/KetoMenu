## Kebabs

4 servings

- 1.1 lb ground beef
- 1 small red onion finely chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- ½ tsp salt
- ½ tsp pepper
- 1 tsp each:
	- smoked paprika
	- cumin
	- chili flakes
	- oregano
	- parsley
- oil for the pan
- for serving:
	- crumbled feta
	- sliced cherry tomatoes
	- greek yogurt

Heat oven to 400º.

Mix all kebab ingredients in a bowl.

Line a sheet pan with baking parchment and oil it.

Transfer mixture to pan, add another sheet of paper and flatten with a spatula or back of a second tray, to about ½ inch thickness.

Remove top layer of paper and use a blunt knife to divide the mixture into 8 strips. Cut about halfway through.

Bake for 12-15 min until browned and cooked through. Optionally broil a bit for the last 5 min to char the top.

Slice the 8 strips all the way through and serve.
