## Lobster

Need a large kettle with a cover.

Add water, 2.5 qts/lobster.

Add 2 tbsp salt/qt water.

Get water boiling.

Weigh lobsters if weight not known.

Add lobsters one at a time, cover and bring back to boil.

Simmer 10 min for first lb, 3 min for each additional lb.

They’re done when you can pull off an antenna easily.
