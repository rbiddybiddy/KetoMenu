## Mushrooms, Morel, Sauteed

- 1-2 cups morel mushrooms
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 clove garlic, sliced
- ¼ tsp each: marjoram, rosemary, thyme, oregano
- splash of white wine, or lemon or lime juice
- splash of cream
- 2 green onions, chopped

Soak morels in water, agitate, and drain. Repeat a few times. Slice mushrooms in half lengthwise.

Heat oil and butter in saucepan on med-high. Add mushrooms & sauté for 1-2 minutes.

Add garlic & spices and sauté for another 1-2 minutes.

Salt liberally, add remaining ingredients, and sauté for another minute or so.
