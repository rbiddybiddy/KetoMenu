## Pizza

Crust:
- 7 oz cheddar, shredded
- 4 oz cream cheese
- 3 eggs
- 3 cloves garlic, pressed
- 1 tsp oregano
- 1 tbsp psyllium husk powder
- ½ cup almond flour

Sauce:
- 6 oz can tomato paste
- 3 cloves garlic, pressed
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp oregano
- 1 tsp basil
- 1 tsp salt
- ¾ cup water

Toppings:
- 8 oz mozzarella, shredded
- pepperoni
- anything

Heat oven to 375º.

Mix crust ingredients, spread into two pie plates and bake 25 min.

Mix sauce ingredients, adding the water last, and simmer on medium to low heat.

Remove crusts from oven & add sauce (NF likes a lot), toppings.

Return to oven & broil 4-5 min on middle rack.
