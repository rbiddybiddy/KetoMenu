## Pork Tenderloin in Creamy Dijon Sauce

4 servings

- 1.3 lb pork tenderloin sliced into 1-inch medallions
- ½ tsp salt
- ½ tsp pepper
- ½ tsp oregano
- ½ tsp paprika
- 1½ tbsp oil
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- ½ cup white wine
- ½ cup chicken stock
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
- ½ cup heavy cream
- ¼ tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 2 tbsp freshly chopped parsley for garnish

Place pork medallions on a plate.

Mix salt, pepper, oregano and paprika and sprinkle both sides of the medallions with it.

Heat oil in large pan over high heat. Cook medallions about 2 min per side, until golden.

Reduce heat to medium, add garlic, stir for 30 seconds, then add wine and stir to deglaze the pan.

Let wine simmer 2 min to reduce slightly, then add chicken stock and simmer 3 min.

Stir in mustard, then cream, then Worcestershire sauce.

Bring back to a boil and simmer gently 2-3 min until sauce thickens slightly.

Sprinkle with the parsley and serve.
