## Salmon Loaf, Cakes or Patties

- 15 oz can salmon
- ¼ cup cream
- 2 beaten eggs
- 1 tsp salt
- ¼ tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp paprika
- 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1 cup diced celery
- ½ cup diced onion
- 2 tsp parsley
- 2 tbsp lemon juice

Combine all ingredients, pour into oiled loaf pan, and bake at 350º for 30 min or until knife comes out clean.
