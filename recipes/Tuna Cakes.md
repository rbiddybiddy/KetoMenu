## Tuna Cakes

- 2 eggs
- ½ a small onion, chopped
- chopped celery
- 1 cup shredded Gouda or cheddar
- 4 oz cream cheese
- (2) 10-ounce cans of tuna, drained

Heat oven to 400º.

Combine all ingredients, shape into 8 or so patties, and bake 10 min at 400º on a parchment-lined baking pan.

Flip & bake 5 more min until golden brown.
